## About

LocalK Digital helps restaurants, cafes, and bakeries translate the quality of what they serve into the online presence they deserve. Founded by two Dallas-based professionals with backgrounds in IT, sales, cybersecurity, and data analysis.

## Services

- **Local SEO & Google Visibility** — Google Business Profile optimization, search rankings, citations, reviews
- **Social Media & Content Creation** — consistent, high-quality brand content
- **Influencer Partnerships** — strategic introductions to trusted local influencers

## Our Process

A simple 3-step growth framework:

1. **Discover** — Free consultation to identify visibility gaps and growth bottlenecks
2. **Build** — Strengthen SEO, social presence, and brand consistency
3. **Grow** — Convert attention into engagement, customers, and repeat business
